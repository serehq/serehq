Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

The short version: We collect only what we need to run Seré. We do not sell your data. We do not share it with advertisers. You can opt out at any time.

1. Who This Policy Applies To

This Privacy Policy applies to all visitors and users of serehq.co, newsletter.serehq.co, partners.serehq.co, and any related Seré properties operated by Seré HQ LLC.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

  • Newsletter subscription: When you subscribe to The Language Gap, we collect your email address via Kit (formerly ConvertKit). You may optionally provide your first name.
  • Contact and inquiry forms: When you reach out via email or contact forms, we collect the information you provide (name, email, message).
  • Scheduling: When you book a call via Calendly, Calendly collects your name, email, and scheduling preferences per their own privacy policy.
  • Site analytics: We may use standard analytics tools to understand site traffic. This data is aggregated and not personally identifiable.

We do not collect sensitive personal information such as health records, financial information, or government IDs.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Send The Language Gap newsletter (if you have subscribed)
  • Respond to inquiries and facilitate booked calls
  • Understand how people use the Site so we can improve it
  • Send transactional emails related to your engagement with Seré

We do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share limited data only with the following service providers for operational purposes:

  • Kit (formerly ConvertKit) — newsletter delivery and list management
  • Calendly — scheduling
  • GitHub Pages — website hosting (no personal data stored)

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Seré, its users, or the public.

5. Email Communications

If you subscribe to The Language Gap newsletter, you will receive weekly emails from Seré. Every email includes an unsubscribe link. You may also opt out by emailing hello@serehq.co. We will honor all unsubscribe requests promptly.

6. Cookies and Tracking

The Site may use cookies or similar technologies for basic analytics. These do not contain personally identifiable information. You can disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting your ability to use the Site.

7. Data Retention

We retain newsletter subscriber data for as long as you remain subscribed. If you unsubscribe, your data is removed from active mailing lists. Inquiry data is retained for a reasonable period for business correspondence purposes.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@serehq.co.

9. Children's Privacy

The Site is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will update the date at the top when we do. Continued use of the Site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Email hello@serehq.co.
